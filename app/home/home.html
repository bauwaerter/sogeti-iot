Empowering survivors of domestic and sexual violence

Mission

To empower victims of domestic violence and sexual assault by providing emergency safe shelter and support services , and to reduce violence in our communities.

Emergency Safe Shelters

Brighter Tomorrows staffs 2 emergency safe shelters in Grand Prairie and Irving in undisclosed locations. Both locations are gated and equipped with security cameras. Our safe shelters can house up to a total of 70 residents (women, children, and men). Clients and their children receive meals, clothing, crisis counseling, advocacy, and safety planning assistance.

Resource and Counseling Services

Counseling services and crisis intervention services are available to shelter and non-shelter clients. Counselors are available Monday - Friday 8am - 5pm. NO APPOINTMENT NECESSARY, during regular business hours. Evening counseling services are available by appointment only.
